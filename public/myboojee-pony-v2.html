What's Inside

Why Mineral?

Chemical sunscreens absorb UV rays through a chemical reaction — meaning potential irritation, especially on sensitive equine skin.

Mineral sunscreens physically reflect UV rays away from the skin. Our powder formula takes it even further — no liquid carriers, no synthetic binders. Just pure protection that goes on light and stays put.

The white residue isn't a flaw — it's proof it's working, and on horses with white markings, it actually brightens their chrome beautifully.

Physical Protection
